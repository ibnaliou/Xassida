1. salaatul lazii fii kulli shahrin wa fii safar
2. hamaanii halaa maahin mahal habsa was safar
3. falaahi bikawnii habda rabbii khadiimahu
4. hajiibun bihi lii jaada mughnin hanin nafar
5. radiitu hanil mawlaa wa han khayri khalqihi
6. halayhi salaamaa man lahu ubtu biz zafar
7. salaatun wa tasliimun mahal bishri sarmadan
8. halaa man bihi muksii mahal amni wal bashar
9. firaariya bil mukhtaari min qablu qaada lii
10. mukuusan wa tabshiran ladal jinni wal bashar
11. rafahtu shukuuran bahda hamdin mukhalladin
12. libaaqin mahaa durril lazii haazahul qadar
13. salaatul lazii adhuuhu habdan lahu bihi
14. halaa man bihi lii qaada safwan bilaa kadar
15. fasaahatu khayril khalqi abdat qasaa-idii
16. wa kawnii bihaa habdan khadiiman lahu zahar
17. rabihtu rabaahan lam yakun qattu misluhu
18. bifadlil lazii kawnii habiiban lahush tahar
19. salaatul lazii lii qaada kawnii khaliilahu
20. halaa man bihi lii tayyabal badwa wal hadar
21. futuuhaatu rabbil harshi anqat mahaa-ibii
22. bijaahil lazii baahat bihi man halaw mudar
23. rabaahiya fii ikhdaami khayril waraa badaa
24. wa fii kulli shahrin zaydu ribhii wa fii safar

1. kawwin liyal yawma nasran hazza mawlaayaa
2. wal takshifil hujba hannii hab liyal aayaa
3. tawwaabu lighfir zunuubii wamhu kulla laghan
4. hanni wa kun liya fii dunyaa wa ukhraayaa
5. bikawnikal waahidal qahhaara rud liya yaa
6. rabbil hidaa waltukhallid fiika bushraa yaa
7. antal ilaahul lazii tuhlii wa tarfahu man
8. qad shi-tahu faltutayyib fiika mah-yaa yaa
9. lakas samaa wal araadii wal hawaa-u mahan
10. qinii azal kulli wal tukhlid mazaayaa yaa
11. lakas salaatiinu turran wal muluuku kazaa
12. atbaa huhum liya rudhum qabla luq-yaa yaa
13. hab lii talaazuma maa tardaahu min hamalin
14. wa min maqaalin wa haqdin hinda  maghnaa yaa
15. lakal baraayaa jamiihan wal bilaa dufakun
16. lii yaa ilaahiya fii daarii wa tuubaa yaa
17. unsur janaabii wa hisnii lii atib humurii
18. bimaa tuhibbu wa ahsin fiika taqwaa yaa
19. ghaniitu bil waahidil qahhaari han zumarin
20. wa kaana lii wa nafaa faqri wa balwaa yaa
21. lahu tawajjahtu zaa shukrin lahu abadan
22. wa artajii minhu maa lam yahwi sharwaa yaa
23. bikawnikal qaadiral qahhaara muqtadiran
24. arjuu talaazuma maa ahwaa bimanjaa yaa
25. naajaytuhu raajiyan an laa yukhayyibanii
26. wa an yuwajjiha lii maa faaqa man jaayaa
27. nawaytu irdaa-ahu irdaa-a man salahuu
28. bahdan siraa fii ilaa ahlii wa suknaa yaa
29. antal qadiirul lazii arjuu biqudratihi
30. hawzis siyaadata zidnii bahda husnaa yaa
31. nabaztu maa kaana hindii suq bikawnika lii
32. dunyaa wa ukhral lazii tardaa birughbaa yaa
33. atil mukuusii bitaahaatin mukhallasatin
34. waktub biqahrika yaa rabbii ghulubba yaa
35. wajjih bihizzika lii hizzan akuunu bihi
36. habdan haziizan wa khallid hanka hutbaa yaa
37. rabbaytanii bil hidaa rud lil jamiiha wa qud
38. nii lil a-hibbati wahmad rabbi mas-haa yaa
39. suq sarmadan liya maa faaqal munaa karaman
40. wajhal kitaabaka fid daarayni marhaa yaa
41. lii suq dawaaman ku-uusal qawmi tarwiyatan
42. waltasqinii waltuhazzim rabbi suq-yaa yaa
43. yaa zaal baqaa-il lazii abqayta lii humurii
44. habdan khadiiman sahiidan wahmi bugh-yaa yaa
45. ilayka wajjahtu wajhii daahiyan malikii
46. falis tajib karaman yaa rabbi dahwaa yaa
47. najjaytanii faltazidnii rabbi tanjiyatan
48. wal tahminii wahmi maa yahwiihi maswaa yaa
49. najjaytanii amsi min hawdii ilaa silahin
50. fal yawma kun lii wa hazzim minka jadwaa yaa 
51. antal hafiizul lazii abghii himaayatahu
52. fakun hafiizii wa zid yaa rabbi taqwaa yaa
53. lakan siraa fiya kasban qudzimaamiya yaa
54. rabbi wa hab liya hamdan rabbi masraa yaa
55. lakartihaa liya zaa jar-yin fasuq gharadii
56. liya haajilan aa-iban wal tahmi majraa yaa
57. hab lii bikawnika wahhaabaa bilaa sababin
58. maa faaqa zanniya wamlaa rabbi mahdaa yaa
59. qud zaa bilutqin wa juudin wakfinii zalalan
60. lakan tihaa-ii ilaahii minka mabdaa yaa
61. wajjahtu fis sabti wajhii laka fii rajabin
62. wa fis sulaasaa-i fii shahbaana maljaa yaa
63. yaa rabbi salli bitasliimin halaa sanadii
64. muhammadin wa bihi qudnii li-arjaa yaa
65. yaa rabbi salli bitasliimin halayhi bilaa
66. haddin mahal aali wal as-haabi manjaa yaa
67. halayhi salli wa sallim bil jamiihi wa kun
68. munawwiran biya azmaanii wa anjaa yaa
69. zidnii huluuman bikashfin laa yufaariqunii
70. yaa man lahu ashtakii waltushki shakwaa yaa
71. yaa khayra mughnin qadaa haajii bilaa kulafin
72. yaa man bikawnika lii faaraqtu balwaa yaa
73. zintul qasaa-ida fiykum shaakiran lakumu
74. bihaa bikum waajahuu lil kashfa wal aayaa

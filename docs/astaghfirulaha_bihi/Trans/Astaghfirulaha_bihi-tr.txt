1. astaghfirul laahal haziima wa atuub
2. ilayhi min zawahirin wa min ghuyuub
3. sa-altuhu bihaqqi wajhil laahi
4. maghfiratan min jumlatil manaahii
5. tubtu lahu min jumlatis saghaa-iri
6. mahal kabaa-iri wa min damaa-iri
7. ghaafiru lighfir kulla maa taqaddamaa
8. wa maa ta-akhkhara wa maa baynahumaa
9. fahhimniyal hilma wa zidnii hilmaa
10. wa hamalan wa adaban wa fahmaa
11. rabbiya lik shifil huluman nafihah
12. wajhal hayaati min shaqaa-in maanihah
13. ikshif liyal asraara wal ghawaamidaa
14. yaa khayra man  kashafa sirran ghaamidaa
15. lijmah jamiiha maa tafarraqa ladaa
16. khattii minal khayri wa saffil khaladaa
17. lii qud ladaa tafakkuril ahzama yaa
18. rabbii bikun waltahsiman qalamiyaa
19. hab liya yaa akramu fit tilaawah
20. khayran kasiiran minka wal halaawah
21. baarik liyal lahumma fii hayaatii
22. wajhal fu-aadii wa tanal aayaatii
23. hab liya kawnii bishra kulli man yatuub
24. wa liz zawaahira abin mahal ghuyuub

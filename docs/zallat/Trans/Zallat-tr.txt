1. zallat lihizzati rabbin anzalas suwaraa
2. jamaahatul khaliq wahwal muhsinus suwaraa
3. wajjahtu wajhii lahu habdan lahu abadan
4. zaa khidmatin lirasuulil laahi kal kubaraa
5. uhdii lahu bihadaa yal madhu naa filatan
6. wa bis salaati bihubit yuurisul kibaraa
7. lahu halayya liwajhil laahi laa lilaghan
8. duyuunu madhin taziidus sabqa wal mumuraa
9. qalib quluubal hidaa yaa rabbi fii humurii
10. linusratii wal tarud lii jumlatal umaraa
11. halayhi salli wa sallim bil kiraami wa hab
12. bihi liyal amna fid daarayni wal busharaa
13. dahawtukal yawma sakhkhii lil hidaa abadan
14. rud lil lahiina wa rud lil jinna wal basharaa
15. taraktu fii rajabin maa anta tahlamuhu
16. faltakfiniihaa wa hab lil aaya was suwara
17. hab lii quhudan bimaa tardaahu min hamalin
18. abqayta lil humra yaa man ahsanas suwaraa

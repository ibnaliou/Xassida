1. ilaa ghayrinaa qad wajjahal kayda was sah-yaa
2. zaluumun wa shaytaanun biman qaada lir rah-yaa
3. hanil laahi wal mukhtaari qad sirtu raadiyaa
4. wa qad kabkabash shaytaana qad baa-a mukhfiyyaa
5. wujuuhu sihaabil muntaqaa waajahul hidaa
6. bitardin wa irdaa-in kamaa admanun naf-yaa
7. zababtul hidaa bil laahi wal muntaqan nabii
8. furaadii wa musnii adbaruu laa zamul harbaa
9. bari-tu liwajhil laahi min kulli mushrikin
10. khaliilan habiiban lil lazii tayyabal mah-yaa
11. ajaabaniyal baaqil lazii laa yuruddu lii
12. kahil mushtaraatad dahra muz qaada lii tayyaa
13. liman zahzahal ahdaa-i bil khiz-yi sarmadaa
14. ilaa ghayriyal hamdul lazii qaada lil bugh-yaa
15. lirabbil lazii laa yusilud dahra lii azan
16. shukuurii wa hamdii taarikan maahawaa nah-yaa
17. huwal laahu war rahmaanu wahwar rahiimu man
18. ilayhin tahaa bil muntaqaa daama mardiyyaa
19. muhammadunal mukhtaaru qad baana kawnuhu
20. ra-iisal waraa man raddahu laazamal ghayyaa
21. nabiyyun rasuulun wal mazaayaa jaliyyatun
22. wa wajjahahu lil khalqi man lam yazal hayyaa
23. azaala bihir rahmaanu maa qad nahal hidaa
24. halayhi salaatul laahi maa jaada wastah-yaa
25. lahu maa nafash shaytaana wad durra wal balaa
26. ilaa ghayrinaa wan qaada lis safwu wal bugh-yaa
27. shakartul lazii ardaa haduwwi muwajjihan
28. ilaa ghayriyash shaytaana bil ya-si manfiyyaa
29. yasuuqu lighayrii man hadaanii bizikrihi
30. lahiinan wa man laa yatlubul fahma wad dir-yaa
31. tawaaful hidaa turran lighayrii faman abaa
32. masaalamatii bit tawbi laa yahtawii had-yaa
33. ajaabaniyal baaqil lazii qaada kah mahaa
34. lighayrii wa lii qaadal munaa laa uraa bagh-yaa
35. nafaa kah lighayrii sarmadan khayru shaafihin
36. yuwajjihu lil baaqii bihish shibha war riyyaa
37. ilaa ghayri hibbil laahi hibbi rasuulihi
38. yamiilul lazii lam yaqbalil birra wal wah-yaa
39. laqad baana lii annal muqaffaa rasuulu man
40. yukhallidu lii sirran yuwalil hidaa khiz-yaa
41. rafahtu sanaa-i lil muqaffaa wa hizbihi
42. kamaa qasda kah nahwii mahaa rabbuhu mah-yaa
43. jazaa-ul lazii lii qiida fii haami daksashin
44. jazaa-u minal maahil hanaa muz mahal khash-yaa
45. yasuuqu lighayril laahu kulla mufannidin
46. tariidan ilaa ghayrii dawaaman bilaa luq-yaa
47. mahaa qasda kah lii man hadaanii birabbihi
48. halayhi salaamaa man bihi qaada lir rah-yaa

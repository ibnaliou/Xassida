1. halal muntaqaa khayril baraayaa muhammadi
2. salaamayka khallid yaa ilaahii bihummadi
3. ayaa maahiyan min qabli haksashin maa asaa
4. lifadlin wa takriimin wa majdin wa suudadi
5. mazaayaa karaamat haddahaa qabliyal malaa
6. wa kalluu wa maa zul ijtihaadi kazidadi
7. hadaa bika rabbii kulla man raama had-yahu
8. halayka salaatul laahi yaa khayra muhtadi
9. kitaabuka lii kanzun wa jaahun wa rifhatun
10. wa man yurdi bil qur-aani mawlaaka yahtadi
11. salaamal lazii  ahlaaka yaa khayra munzalin
12. halaa khayri mabhuusin ilal khalqi murshidi
13. shakartu halaykal laahi shukran yaziidunii
14. wa man bika yanhush shukra yaa zikru  yurshadi
15. falaahul lazii yatluuka yabqaa mukhalladan
16. ilal jannatil hul-yaa bighayrit taraddudi
17. yaqiinii yaqiinii tarkakad dahra yaa hudan
18. mahaa bika rabbii duuna raddin takhaddudii
19. rabbaahul lazii yatluuka rinhu mubashsharin
20. wa man bika yabghil fawza yaa zikru yas-hadi
21. bikal laahu ahtaanii amaanan minal azaa
22. ladaa kulli man yadnuu wa kulli mubahhadi
23. yaquudu liyal baaqii bikas su-la aaminan
24. wa man bika yabghin nasra yaa zikru yunjadi
25. halimtu yaqiinan annakal khayru wal hudaa
26. wa man yabghi majdan khaalidaa bika yumjadi
27. aruumu bikal haajaati min khayri munzilin
28. wa lii bika haajin qad qadaa duura mufsidi
29. lighayrin tahat dunyaa wa ukhraa madarratun
30. kafaanii bilaa luq-yaa bikal laahu hussadii
31. liyal laahu yubdil ghayba mahmaa aradtuhu
32. bikal yawma yaa qur-aana rabbii wa fii ghadi
33. izaa rumtu min rabbii bikas su-la niltuhu
34. bikal laahu saffaa lii baqaa-a bi-arghadi
35. wa ristuka yaa qur-aana rabbii bifadlihi
36. faman lam yuwaa fiqnii lighayriya yutradi
37. widaadii libaaqin anzalaz zikra muhkaman
38. halaa man bihi lam yanhunii zuu tamarrudi
39. liwajhika hannii salli rabbii musalliman
40. halaa khayri khalqil laahi turran muhammadi

1. ajaabanii khayru baaqin fiihi qad taabaa
2. kullii wa raqqaa bimaa lii qaada aqtaabaa
3. liwajhi baaqin mahaa qasdal mabiihi lanaa
4. faaraqtuhu wahwa laj wal amlu maa khaabaa
5. bihtul lazii laysa yanhuunii wa lii badalii
6. wa lii yukhalliduhu man kabba sakhkhaabaa
7. ilaa siwaayal hidaa tanhuu bimaa mahahum
8. wa bii yusaffii ilaahid dahra albaabaa
9. quluubu man lam yahibbuunii halaa wajalin
10. walaa yulaaquuna maaraamu walal baabaa
11. yanqaadu lii kullu maaraamal kiraamu munan
12. wal laahu raqqaa bimaa lii qaada aqtaabaa

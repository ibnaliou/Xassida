1. ahuuzu bil laahi min maylii lighayri ridaa
2. wa min suduudiya hammaa sunna aw furidaa
3. halayya kawniya habdal laahi fii abadin
4. mahal kitaabil lazii man raddahu quridaa
5. wasiilatii habdahul mukhtaaru sayyidunaa
6. wahwal lazii man abaa taghdiimahu khufidaa
7. zaakash shafiihul lazii arjuu shafaahatahu
8. dunyaa wa ukhraa walaa abghii bihi hiwadaa
9. baayahtuhu bifu-aadii hiira lam tarahu
10. haynii wa akhdimuhu lil laahi muftaridaa
11. aslamtu wajhii lirabbii bin nabii abadan
12. mahal kitaabil lazii qad saaqa lil gharadaa
13. lil laahi kulliyyatii habdan lahu abadan
14. walaa utaawihu man qad jaara aw rafadaa
15. lil laahi qad tubtu fii zal yawmi bidahii
16. wa kulli hahdin li-ajlish shakki qad nuqidaa
17. huwal hafiizul lazii qad kaana lii wa hamaa
18. kullii wa azhab hannil karba wal maradaa
19. mahawta an yaqsidash shaytaanu fii abadin
20. ilaa jihaatii fahahdi laysa muntaqidaa
21. nahuuzu bil laahi fii sirrin wa fii halanin
22. min sharri ibliisa haysu anghadda aw rakadaa
23. ijhal bihifzika lii hisnan yaghii dararan
24. yaa khayra man kaffa waqbad hanniyal fadadaa
25. shakawtu haalii ilaykal yawma murtajiyan
26. artagh-harad dahra lii man qalbuhu maridaa
27. shakkii wa waswasatii han qalbiyan sarafaa
28. fakammilanniya waltuslih liyar rabadaa
29. ya-ummunii qitlunaa ibliisu zaa hiyalin
30. wa anta rabbii wa liyyii fakfinil haradaa
31. tayyib baqaa-ii bi-amnin was salaamati min
32. ibliisa waltakfiniihid dahra irnnahadaa
33. utruduhu hanniya yaa rabbii wa maa mahahu
34. dunyaa wa ukhraa bitakh-yiibin mataa haradaa
35. nazzif fu-aadii minal agh-yaari zaa warahin
36. yaa khayra man rayna qalbii bil hudaa rahadaa
37. isrif jamiihal ahaadii bin nabii abadan
38. hannii bitaskhiirihim yaa khayra man khafadaa
39. lakal khazaa-inu faftah lii wazid busharii
40. waltakfinii kulla man yaatii liyahtaridaa
41. rajawtu sarfaka hannii kulla mafsadatin
42. haqqiq rajaa-ii wa lik shif sirra maa ghamudaa
43. jismii wa ahli wa awlaadii lakum abadan
44. wa diihatan fakfinad daarayni suu-a qadaa
45. yaa man lahul amru kun lii wahminii abadan
46. wa salli da-ban halaa man hubbuhu furidaa
47. muhammadin summa sallimi mah jamaahatihi
48. wajhal bihi kulla maa minnii yajii-u ridaa
